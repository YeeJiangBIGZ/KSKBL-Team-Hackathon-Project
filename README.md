# Beneath the Cart

## Project Description

Beneath the Cart is an interactive environmental awareness app created for BasisHacks 2026. The app helps users discover the hidden environmental costs behind everyday products such as plastic water bottles, fast fashion shirts, disposable coffee cups, takeout containers, and smartphone upgrades.

On the surface, these products may seem cheap, convenient, or harmless. Beneath the surface, they can involve pollution, carbon emissions, water use, waste, mining, transportation impact, and other hidden environmental problems.

The user selects a product from a dropdown menu, clicks a button to reveal its hidden cost, and receives a hidden cost score, risk level, explanation, main impacts, eco-friendly alternative, and small action they can take.

## Theme Connection

The BasisHacks 2026 theme is "Beneath the Surface." This project connects to the theme by revealing the hidden environmental costs behind ordinary products.

Most people only see the surface of a product: its price, convenience, or appearance. Beneath the surface, the product may have hidden impacts such as pollution, waste, water use, carbon emissions, and resource extraction.

Beneath the Cart encourages users to look deeper at their everyday choices and understand the hidden systems behind consumption.

## How to Use the App

1. Open the public Code.org App Lab demo link.
2. Click **Start**.
3. Select a product from the dropdown menu.
4. Click **Reveal Hidden Cost**.
5. Read the hidden cost report.
6. Click **See Better Choice** to view a more eco-friendly alternative.
7. Explore the learning and theme explanation screens.

## Data

The project uses a CSV data table called `HiddenCost`. The app reads product information using App Lab's `getColumn()` function.

Main data columns include:

- `product_name`
- `surface_description`
- `hidden_environmental_cost`
- `overall_hidden_cost_score`
- `risk_level`
- `key_hidden_impacts`
- `eco_friendly_alternative`
- `small_user_action`
